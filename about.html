hello!

My name is Evie (Eh-vee) Larkins and I’m a Visual Communications student at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago graduating in 2023 (BFA). I strive for organization and balance within my work, and enjoy creating hierarchies with typography, grids, and color. I also love combining design with other practices, and I’m not afriad to tackle different textures, patterns, or media.
