Cincinnati Zoo & Botantical Garden Identity System

During the course of a semester, I created a new mark and system for an existing corporation. I chose the Cincinnati Zoo and Botanical Garden, and through extensive research decided to take the system in a new direction revolving around the ideas of nature, habitats, conservation, and research. I created a mark in the form of a container to display the variety of plants and animals the zoo has to offer as well as to create variety within the system.
